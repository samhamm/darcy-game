Mr. Darcy's Guessing Game

Description

Mr. Darcy is just a cute dumb dog, but he has a thing for guessing games and numbers. In his little bitty peanut-sized brain, he is thinking of a number. Mr. Darcy likes numbers. And rats. Tasty.

Instructions

  1. Your task is to guess Mr. Darcy's number.
  2. You can make a total of three guesses
  3. After each wrong guess, Mr. Darcy will give you a hint
  4. If you do not guess his number after three tries, Mr. Darcy will mock you.
  5. If you guess his number, Mr. Darcy will give you a big wet sloppy doggie kiss.
